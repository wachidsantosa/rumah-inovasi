About

Rumah Inovasi is a gathering place for all people who pay attention to the development of Digital Creative Industries in Indonesia.

Rumah Inovasi was established on December 16, 2008. MIKTI activities are focused on efforts to build the sustainable and competitive ecosystem for the growth of Digital Creative Industries in Indonesia.

Digital Creative Industries including: Game Industry, Education Software Industry, Digital Music Industry, Animation Industry, Software & Application Industry

Vision

Become the main driver of Indonesia’s Digital Creative Industries in order to create a competitive and sustainable industry.

Mission

Encourage the creation of sustainable Digital Creative Industry Ecosystem in Indonesia. Support the growth of Creativepreneur community (Digital Creative based Entrepreneur) which has DIGITAL culture.
